Welcome to Disaster Assistance Management System

Founded 2021

  • DAMS is a partially automated system that allows individuals to request for aid and respond to natural and man-made disasters around the globe.
  • To sign up, click here.
  • You may register as a requester if you have experienced a disaster and are in need of aid. To request for aid, you can navigate to your dashboard.
  • You may also register as a responder. To respond to a request for aid, you can click below to see all current emergencies or search our database by keyword.
  • You may also make a pledge as a responder by navigating to your dashboard.
  • Requests and responses will be automatically matched where possible, and also manually matched by our dedicated team of administrators.
